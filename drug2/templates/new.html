About DDI System

Drug-Drug Interaction (DDI) refers to the pharmacological or clinical impact of one drug on the effectiveness or toxicity of another drug when both drugs are administered together.

DDIs are an important consideration in medication management, as they can significantly affect patient outcomes. As such, healthcare providers need to be aware of potential DDIs when prescribing medications and should carefully consider the risks and benefits of each medication.
